About Us

In 1999 Meenakshi established her dance academy KALAKSHTRA dance Academy with the sole purpose of training and transferring this rich tradition to numerous students , thereby preserving this unique art form . In the last 20 years over 750 students have trained at Kalakshetra and 75 students have become active performers. Today the school is recognized as a premier institution in the field of Bharatanatyam . It is empanelled with the Government of India and the Indian Council for Cultural Relations whereby many exchange foreign students avail government scholarships and train at Kalakshetra has further launched live online lessons by experienced teachers trained by Mrs Meenakshi. Individual online lessons cost between INR 800 - 1500 per 45 minute lesson based on teacher skill levels.
